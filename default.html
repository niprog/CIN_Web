Who are Cavaliers in Need?

Cavaliers in Need are a small and growing rescue group of volunteers and Cavalier King Charles Spaniel lovers who came together in 2013. Our members in Northern Ireland provide foster homes for dogs in need. We fundraise to support this work and the group also meet on a regular basis for a bit of fun for us and the dogs!

What can I do to help?

You can help by fostering, transporting, fundraising and supporting what we do. Please get in touch to see how you can get involved
